Feminist art is an artistic movement that highlights the contributions of women in the field of art and aims to denounce the inequalities and discrimination suffered by women in the arts. This movement has taken the scale in the 1960s and 1970s, along with the women’s liberation movement.

Several artists have contributed to the feminist art movement, Among them are Luchita Hurtado, Miriam Schapiro, Joan Brown and Harmonia Rosales.
